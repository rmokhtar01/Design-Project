Action movies have been something to keep us all entertained for decades, to see stunts that are impossible to do in real life and to escape reality to forget our problems. Action movies have created a world of imagination for people of all ages.


The first five movies shown are the top rated action movies of all time:




Up first on the action list is Die Hard A true classic, as we call it. Released on july 22nd 1988 and was directed by John McTiernan and stars Bruce Willis Alan Rickman. New York City policeman John McClane is visiting his estranged wife and two daughters on Christmas Eve. He joins her at a holiday party in the headquarters of the Japanese-owned business she works for. But the festivities are interrupted by a group of terrorists who take over the exclusive high-rise, and everyone in it. Very soon McClane realizes that there's no one to save the hostages, but him. There's no other movie to call a true action movie other than Die Hard, with the amazing usage of the weapons and iconic lines to make us laugh through the punching and fighting in the background. The amazing performances done by our main star heroes, and villains. Alan Rickman does an amazing job portraying our bad guy for this film and him and Bruce Willis made this movie what it known for today, with everything it needs to be an action movie, it succeed tremendously and we will cherish it forever, if you like big action with good comedy to balance it out then this movie was made just for you!



Next on the critic's list is Indiana Jones Specifically Raiders of the Lost ark. Released June 21st 1981 and was directed by the amazing Steven Spielberg and stars Harrison Ford and Karen Allen. In 1936, archaeologist and adventurer Indiana Jones is hired by the U.S. government to find the Ark of the Covenant before the Nazis can obtain its awesome powers. One of the best franchises there is to date, the Indiana Jones movies climbed to the top of the scale for over 4 decades and finally landed a spot as one of the most recognizable and memorable movies to date. Out of the films I feel Raider's of the Lost Ark is the best, and that's why it's on this list. As amazing as it is, the adventures of our beloved main character, Indiana Jones are still going on to this day, with a new film added to the franchise released just in the year 2023! In Raiders of the lost Ark, Harrison Ford participated in some of the most memorable scenes in cinematic history, with incredible stunts being pulled, no second of this film will ever be forgotten, and if you enjoy more of an adventure type action movie, this movie is definetly for you.



In third place comes Kill Bill A film directed by the phenomenal Quentin Tarantino, released on October 10th 2003 and stars Uma Thurman, Lucy Liu and David Carradine. A former assassin, known simply as The Bride, wakes from a coma four years after her jealous ex-lover Bill attempts to murder her on her wedding day. Fueled by an insatiable desire for revenge, she vows to get even with every person who contributed to the loss of her unborn child, her entire wedding party, and four years of her life. After devising a hit list, The Bride sets off on her quest, enduring unspeakable injury and unscrupulous enemies. This movie isn't just about the guts, gore and blood being spladdered around the screen, but rather has a very deep plot and lore to it all. The amazingly crafted fight scenes bring both volumes together into this parallel univers to kill bill. The beautiful acting performances given by each actor shows true talent and gives us such an entertaining movie to watch, no matter how many fight scenes, it won't ever get boring. If you enjoy the blood and gore of action movies then I suggest you watch this masterpiece right away!



Next up is The Matrix Directed by Lana Wachowski and Lilly Wachowski, it was released on March 31st 1999 and stars Keanu Reeves, Carrie-Anne Moss and Laurence Fishburne. Neo believes that Morpheus, an elusive figure considered to be the most dangerous man alive, can answer his question, what is the Matrix? Neo is contacted by Trinity, a beautiful stranger who leads him into an underworld where he meets Morpheus. They fight a brutal battle for their lives against a cadre of viciously intelligent secret agents. It is a truth that could cost Neo something more precious than his life. As famous as this movie is, The Matrix is an astonishing film, with it's unqiue fight/battle scenes and interesting concepts to show it's originality towards other films being released at the time. The world of The Matrix is as original as it sounds, being one of the most recognizable films to date, the franchise had been so ahead of it's time with the crazy and unique battles shown with the amazing performances given by each actor. If you love the sci-fi/action type of movie, this is a big suggestion! It's won awards like the Empress Award for Best Film, Academy Award for Best Visual Effects and MTV Movie & TV Award for Best Fight.



Last on this list is Terminator Directed by James Cameron and stars Arnold Schwarzenegg and Linda Hamilton. Disguised as a human, a cyborg assassin known as a Terminator travels from 2029 to 1984 to kill Sarah Connor. Sent to protect Sarah is Kyle Reese, who divulges the coming of Skynet, an artificial intelligence system that will spark a nuclear holocaust. Sarah is targeted because Skynet knows that her unborn son will lead the fight against them. With the virtually unstoppable Terminator in hot pursuit, she and Kyle attempt to escape. As amazing as this movie sounds, watching is just as incredible. As the movie rose to fame it was obvious sequels would be dropping, and that's exactly what happened. The Terminator franchise gave a name to itself and created films that everyone must watch, with it's eccentric action fights and the interesting concepts, action movie lovers will reccomend this film to anyone new who crosses their path. It is a must watch even for people who don't have action as one of their favorite genres, but it is very worth it. It's won awards such as the Satrun Award for Best Scienc/Fiction film, an Oscar for Best Sound, an Oscar for Best Visual Effects and much more.



The next 5 movies demonstrates my personal favorite action movies of all time:




The first movie on my list is Spanish Judges Directed by Oz Scott, released October 16th 2000 and stars Matthew Lillard, Vicent D'Onofrio and Valeria Golino. A pair of priceless antique pistols is the prize sought by a sometime con man and his lowlife comrades. But this film shows that you shouldn't trust everyone you meet. As underground as this movie is, it holds such a sacred place in my heart. With the interesting point of views registered in this movie, it took me some time to fully understand and appreciate this film, after a few rewatches, I fully understood and came around to this masterpiece of a film. Every fight scene had me gasping to see who would survive, and who would survive the ones after that. With an amazing plot twist, no characters aren't likeable and everyone will get at least one laugh out of you. It's idea of revenge, greediness and obsession to get what we want is so pure and true, some character just won't take no for an answer. Although most reviews for this movie aren't always positive, it didn't stop me from reviewing it and going agaisnt the comments critics had been posting. This movie isn't for everyone, but this webpage wouldn't have been me if I didn't put my actual opinions on it.



My second place belongs to Charlie's Angels Released on October 22nd 2000, was directed by McG and stars Cameron Diaz, Lucy Liu and Drew Barrymore. A trio of elite private investigators armed with the latest in high-tech tools, high-performance vehicles, martial arts techniques and an array of disguises unleash their state-of-the-art skills on land, sea and air to track down a kidnapped billionaire-to-be and keep his top-secret voice-identification software out of lethal hands. Growing up, this was one of first movies I had ever seen, and I instanty fell in love with it and the actors invloved. It holds an important place in my childhood, and growing up I still watch all 3 main actresses movies till this day. With amazing and eye catching fight scenes, the trio included make an unstoppable team and Charlie's Angels is a movie for literally anyone, the perfect balance of comedy and action will answer your question "What movie am I gonna watch?" It's won awards such as MTV Award for Best Dance Sequence, Blockbuster Entertainement Award for Favorite Actress, Kids Choice award for Favourite Movie Actress and more.



In third place is Mr and Mrs. Smith Directed by Doug Liman, released June 7th 2005 and stars Brad Pitt and Angelina Jolie. John and Jane Smith, a couple in a stagnating marriage, live a deceptively dull existence. However, each has been hiding a secret from the other: they are assassins working for adversarial agencies. When they are both assigned to kill the same target, Benjamin Danz (Adam Brody), the truth comes to the surface. Finally free from their cover stories, they discover that they have been assigned to kill each other, sparking a series of explosive attacks. As incredible as this movie sounds, it is just as amazing to watch, the little twists we find out add fun and comedy, the perfect balance for a great action movie. With great performances shown by each actor, the amusement and adrenaline in your body while watching this movie because you are so curious to see what is gonna happen next is impeccable. Everytime I bring this movie up in a conversation, people always call it the movie that Angelina Jolie stole Brad Pitt from Jennifer Aniston, but I believe the movie truly deserves all the fuss it gets and some things shouldn't overshadow the brilliance of this film, it's won awards like the MTV Movie & TV Award for Best Fight, Teen Choice Award for Choice Movie: Rumble, Teen Choice Award for Choice Movie: Liar and more.



Second to last place is Kill Bill Which we've already discussed, but you can never get enough of it! The intense fight scenes makes your heart stop beating and is just breath taking. Along with the great outfits worn by our lead character, really gives her a distinct look throughout the movie. The great stunts done by everyone is just crazy and done so well, not to mention the ending had me very shocked, and if you haven't seen it yet, it is one of my major suggestions for action lovers.



Last on my list is John Wick All 4 movies were directed by Chad Stahelski, the first one was released October 24th 2014 and the last was March 24th 2023 and stars Keanu Reeves. Legendary assassin John Wick retired from his violent career after marrying the love of his life. Her sudden death leaves John in deep mourning. When sadistic mobster Iosef Tarasov and his thugs steal John's prized car and kill the puppy that was a last gift from his wife, John unleashes the remorseless killing machine within and seeks vengeance. Meanwhile, Iosef's father, John's former colleague, puts a huge bounty on John's head. In my opinion, this franchise had been curated perfectly for Keanu Reeves, his amazing action acting skills make the role of John Wick. It's phenomenal fight scenes make this movie what it is, also with the deep backstory involved make you root for the main character. All the visuals with the eccentric colors make the movie have such a spectacular ambiance, if you love the fighting but also deep motivations, I 100% suggest this film to you!
